1. Problem Statement & Solution

Project Lazarus addresses the challenge of Medical Forensic Recovery. In scenarios where patient data is scrambled or partially lost, our engine reconstructs the "Medical Truth" by correlating parity-based telemetry with demographic records and decrypting prescriptions using age-relative cryptographic shifts.

2. Team Composition

Team coolie hackers consists of 4 members, adhering to the hackathon's 2-4 member registration rule. This project represents our single problem statement submission for evaluation.

3. AI Implementation & Justification

AI was utilized to optimize the Forensic Reconstruction Engine. Specifically, we used Large Language Models to:

  • Generate optimized Linear Interpolation algorithms for SpO2 data recovery.
  • Refactor the Parity Sync logic for O(1) time complexity using Map-based lookups.
  • Design the Tailwind-based Medical UI for high-density information display.

Justification: AI integration allowed our team to focus on the high-level forensic logic and data integrity while ensuring the underlying implementation was performant and visually professional.
